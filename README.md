# 📊 Financial Performance Dashboard


## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Financial Performance Dashboard** built using Microsoft Excel.  
The goal of this dashboard is to analyze company financial performance by examining revenue trends, expenses, profit distribution, and product category performance.

The dashboard helps management quickly understand key financial metrics and identify business opportunities through data visualization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
A company wants to evaluate its financial health and answer the following key questions:

- How has **revenue changed over time**?
- Are **expenses growing faster than revenue**?
- Which **regions generate the most profit**?
- Which **product categories contribute the most profit**?
- Are there **seasonal patterns in revenue and profit**?

The dashboard provides insights that support **data-driven business decisions**.

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs
The dashboard includes key performance indicators to summarize financial performance:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Profit**
- **Total Expenses**
- **Profit Margin**

These KPIs allow quick evaluation of overall company performance.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend
A line chart that shows revenue trends across months and years to identify seasonal patterns and business growth.

### 2️⃣ Revenue vs Expenses
A comparison chart that helps determine whether expenses are increasing faster than revenue.

### 3️⃣ Profit by Region
A column chart that highlights the regions contributing the most to overall profit.

### 4️⃣ Profit by Category
A pie chart showing the contribution of different product categories to total profit.

### 5️⃣ Monthly Profit Trend
An area chart illustrating how profit changes throughout the year.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

• The **East region generates the highest profit**, indicating strong market performance in that region.

• **Office Supplies and Technology categories contribute the largest share of total profit**, making them key revenue drivers.

• Revenue trends indicate **seasonal peaks during certain months**, suggesting fluctuating demand patterns.
